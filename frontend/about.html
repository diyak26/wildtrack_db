About WildTrack DB

WildTrack DB is an advanced wildlife conservation and tracking platform built to support national parks, wildlife sanctuaries, and forest organizations. It provides modern, data-driven tools to monitor animals, protect habitats, prevent poaching, and make informed conservation decisions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to safeguard wildlife by providing an intelligent system that delivers accurate tracking, health monitoring, threat alerts, and conservation activity insights. We aim to protect endangered species, restore habitats, and empower conservation teams with reliable, actionable data.

Why WildTrack DB?

  • ✔️ Centralized wildlife data management
  • ✔️ Real-time GPS movement tracking
  • ✔️ Medical and health record monitoring
  • ✔️ Threat alerts for early detection
  • ✔️ Conservation activity tracking
  • ✔️ Habitat and resource zone monitoring
