About WildTrack DB

WildTrack DB is a comprehensive wildlife conservation and tracking system designed to empower forest officials, researchers, and NGOs with modern tools for monitoring, protecting, and preserving wildlife.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a unified platform that enables transparent and efficient wildlife conservation through technology. We aim to support biodiversity preservation and protect endangered species through accurate tracking, health monitoring, and real-time threat detection.

Why WildTrack DB?

  • ✔️ Centralized wildlife data management
  • ✔️ Real-time GPS movement tracking
  • ✔️ Medical and health record monitoring
  • ✔️ Threat alerts for early detection
  • ✔️ Conservation activity tracking
  • ✔️ Habitat and resource zone monitoring

Who Is It For?

This system is developed for:

Forest Department
Researchers & Biologists
Wildlife NGOs
Conservation Volunteers
